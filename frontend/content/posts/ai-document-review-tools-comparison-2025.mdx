## Frequently Asked Questions

### What are the best AI document review tools for law firms?

The best AI document review tools depend on your firm size and e-discovery needs. **Relativity** excels for large firms requiring enterprise-grade e-discovery capabilities. **DISCO** offers the best cloud-native experience with modern interface. **Everlaw** provides superior AI-powered analysis and collaboration features. **Logikcull** is ideal for small to mid-size firms wanting simplified e-discovery.

### How do AI document review tools work?

AI document review tools use machine learning algorithms to automatically classify, tag, and analyze large document sets. They identify relevant documents, detect privileged information, and predict document relevance through predictive coding. AI tools learn from human reviewer decisions to improve accuracy over time, significantly reducing manual review time and costs.

### Which AI document review tool is best for small firms?

**Logikcull** is the best choice for small firms due to its simplified interface, competitive pricing ($25-50 per user per month), and ease of use. **DISCO** also works well for small firms with its cloud-native architecture and modern user experience. Both platforms provide excellent value and require minimal technical expertise for implementation.

### How much do AI document review tools cost?

AI document review tool pricing varies by platform and usage. **Relativity** costs $200-500 per user per month, **DISCO** ranges from $150-400 per user per month, **Everlaw** costs $100-300 per user per month, and **Logikcull** offers the most affordable option at $25-50 per user per month. Small firms can expect to spend $200-1,000 monthly, while large firms may invest $5,000-50,000 monthly.

### Are AI document review tools secure for sensitive documents?

Yes, reputable AI document review tools implement enterprise-grade security measures including end-to-end encryption, SOC 2 Type II compliance, and advanced access controls. **Relativity** offers the highest security standards with enterprise-grade compliance, while **DISCO**, **Everlaw**, and **Logikcull** provide strong security suitable for most law firms. Always verify vendor security certifications before implementation.

### How do I implement AI document review in my practice?

Successful AI document review implementation requires careful planning, comprehensive training, and gradual rollout. Start with a pilot project using a small document set, train key users on platform features, develop firm-specific workflows, and gradually expand to larger projects. Most implementations take 6-10 weeks from planning to full deployment.

### What's the ROI of AI document review tools?

AI document review tools typically deliver 300-500% ROI^7 through time savings, accuracy improvements, and reduced review costs. Firms save 60% in review time, achieve 95% accuracy in privilege detection, and can process 5-10x more documents with the same staff. Most firms see positive ROI within 2-3 months of implementation.

### Do AI document review tools integrate with case management?

Yes, most AI document review tools offer extensive integration capabilities with case management systems. **Relativity** provides the most comprehensive integrations with legal technology ecosystems, **DISCO** integrates well with modern cloud platforms, **Everlaw** offers strong collaboration features, and **Logikcull** provides basic integration capabilities. Check specific integration requirements before selecting a platform.

### How accurate are AI document review tools?

Modern AI document review tools achieve 90-98% accuracy in document classification and privilege detection, significantly outperforming manual review accuracy of 75-85%. **Relativity** and **Everlaw** typically provide the highest accuracy rates, while **DISCO** and **Logikcull** offer good accuracy for most document review needs. Accuracy improves over time as the AI learns from human reviewer decisions.
